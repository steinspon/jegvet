Calculation information

Assumptions used in this calculator:

  • Target dose volume window: 0.05 ml to 0.50 ml
  • Water additions are tested in whole ml increments (1 ml steps)
  • Concentration (mg/ml) = tablet strength (mg) / water added (ml)
  • Dose volume (ml) = required dose (mg) / concentration (mg/ml)
  • Only options within the target dose volume window are displayed
