# Post-Operative Home Care Script

## Goal
Use this script framework when discharging routine surgery patients so instructions are consistent across staff.

## Script Outline
- Explain expected behavior in first 24 hours.
- Review pain medication timing in plain language.
- Discuss e-collar compliance and incision monitoring.
- Confirm activity restriction period.

## Call-Back Triggers For Owners
- Ongoing vomiting or refusal to eat >24 hours.
- Incision swelling, discharge, bleeding, or opening.
- Excessive lethargy, pale gums, or breathing concerns.

## Follow-Up Communication
1. Schedule planned check before discharge.
2. Send handout link by SMS/email.
3. Document owner understanding in patient record.
