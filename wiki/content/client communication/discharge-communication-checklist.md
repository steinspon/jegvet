# Discharge Communication Checklist

## Key Messages To Owner
- Explain expected appetite, energy, and incision appearance over the first 48 hours.
- Confirm medication name, dose, and exact timing in plain language.
- Clarify when to call the clinic versus emergency services.

## Owner Read-Back Prompt
Ask the owner to repeat back:
1. Medication schedule.
2. Activity restrictions.
3. Urgent warning signs.
