# Feline CKD Follow-Up Checklist

## Purpose
Standardize recheck visits for chronic kidney disease cats to improve consistency in monitoring and owner guidance.

## At Each Follow-Up Visit
- Update appetite, thirst, urine output, and body weight.
- Evaluate hydration and body condition score.
- Confirm owner adherence to renal diet and medications.
- Review home quality-of-life notes.

## Monitoring Items
1. Body weight trend.
2. Blood pressure.
3. Renal panel and electrolytes as scheduled.
4. Urinalysis and urine protein trend when indicated.

## Owner Education Reminders
- Encourage daily water intake strategies.
- Explain realistic goals: stability, comfort, and slower progression.
- Give clear triggers for urgent reassessment.
