# Exotics: Small Mammal Medicine

## Norsk

Denne seksjonen dekker klinisk medisin for småpattedyr som vanligvis holdes som kjæledyr eller i laboratoriesammenheng, med fokus på marsvin, chinchilla, mus, rotte, gerbil og hamster.

### Arter som dekkes

- Marsvin (*Cavia porcellus*)
- Chinchilla (*Chinchilla lanigera*)
- Mus (*Mus musculus*)
- Rotte (*Rattus norvegicus*)
- Gerbil (*Meriones unguiculatus*)
- Hamster (syrisk: *Mesocricetus auratus*; kinesisk: *Cricetulus griseus*)

### Referanser

- BSAVA Manual of Rodents and Ferrets, E. Keeble and A. Meredith, BSAVA, 2009
- Exotic Animal Formulary, 3rd Edition, James W. Carpenter. Elsevier Publications, W.B. Saunders, 2004
- Ferrets, Rabbits and Rodents: Clinical Medicine and Surgery, 2nd Ed. Eds Quesenberry and Carpenter. W.B. Saunders, 2007

## English

This section covers clinical medicine for small mammals commonly kept as pets or in laboratory settings, with a focus on guinea pigs, chinchillas, mice, rats, gerbils and hamsters.

### Species covered

- Guinea pig (*Cavia porcellus*)
- Chinchilla (*Chinchilla lanigera*)
- Mouse (*Mus musculus*)
- Rat (*Rattus norvegicus*)
- Gerbil (*Meriones unguiculatus*)
- Hamster (Syrian: *Mesocricetus auratus*; Chinese: *Cricetulus griseus*)

### References

- BSAVA Manual of Rodents and Ferrets, E. Keeble and A. Meredith, BSAVA, 2009
- Exotic Animal Formulary, 3rd Edition, James W. Carpenter. Elsevier Publications, W.B. Saunders, 2004
- Ferrets, Rabbits and Rodents: Clinical Medicine and Surgery, 2nd Ed. Eds Quesenberry and Carpenter. W.B. Saunders, 2007
