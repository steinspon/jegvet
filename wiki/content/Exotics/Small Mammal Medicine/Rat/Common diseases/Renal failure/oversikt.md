# Renal failure

## Norsk

Kronisk interstitiell nefritt er vanlig hos aldrende rotter. Tegn: vekttap, redusert appetitt, polydipsi, polyuri, cystitt. Renal fibrose, glomerulosklerose og amyloidose er også beskrevet. Diagnose: urinanalyse (protein, blod, sylindre, celler) og biokjemi. Behandling: væskediurese, anabole steroider for å bremse progresjonen, lavproteindiett (rotter er altetende), sørg for konstant tilgang til ferskt vann, unngå stressende situasjoner.

## English

Chronic interstitial nephritis is common in ageing rats. Signs: weight loss, reduced appetite, polydipsia, polyuria, cystitis. Renal fibrosis, glomerulosis and amyloidosis have also been reported. Diagnosis: urinalysis (protein, blood, casts, cells) and biochemistry. Treatment: fluid diuresis, anabolic steroids to slow progression, low protein diet (rats are omnivores), ensure constant access to fresh water, avoid stressful situations.
