# Renal failure

## Norsk

Kronisk interstitiell nefritt forekommer hos aldrende gnagere, inkludert mus. Tegn: vekttap, redusert matlyst, polydipsi, polyuri, cystitt. Diagnostikk: urinanalyse (protein, blod, sylindre, celler) og blodprøver. Behandling: væskediurese, anabole steroider for å bremse progresjonen, lavproteindiett (mus er altetere), sikre konstant tilgang på ferskt vann, unngå stressende situasjoner.

## English

Chronic interstitial nephritis occurs in ageing rodents including mice. Signs: weight loss, reduced appetite, polydipsia, polyuria, cystitis. Diagnosis: urinalysis (protein, blood, casts, cells) and biochemistry. Treatment: fluid diuresis, anabolic steroids to slow progression, low protein diet (mice are omnivores), ensure constant access to fresh water, avoid stressful situations.
