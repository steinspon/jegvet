# Skin neoplasia

## Norsk

Hudneoplasi er sjelden hos mus. Plateepitelkarsinom er den hyppigst rapporterte typen. Dermale fibromer, fibrosarkomer og mesenkymale tumorer er også rapportert.

## English

Skin neoplasia is rare in mice. Squamous cell carcinoma is the most frequently reported type. Dermal fibromas, fibrosarcomas and mesenchymal tumours have also been reported.
