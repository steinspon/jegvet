# Common diseases

## Norsk

Vanlige sykdomskategorier hos hamstre:

- Bakteriell hudsykdom
- Kardiorespiratorisk sykdom
- Ektoparasitter
- Endokrin sykdom
- Miljø- og atferdsmessige tilstander
- Sopphudsykdom
- Hodedreining (head tilt)
- Diverse tilstander (inkludert pyometra)
- Nyresvikt
- Hudneoplasi
- Viral hudsykdom

## English

Common disease categories in hamsters:

- Bacterial skin disease
- Cardiorespiratory disease
- Ectoparasites
- Endocrine disease
- Environmental and behavioural conditions
- Fungal skin disease
- Head tilt
- Miscellaneous conditions (including pyometra)
- Renal failure
- Skin neoplasia
- Viral skin disease
