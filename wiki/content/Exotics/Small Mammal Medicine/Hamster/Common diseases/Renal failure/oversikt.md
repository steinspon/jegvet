# Renal failure

## Norsk

Kronisk interstitiell nefritt forekommer hos aldrende gnagere inkludert hamstre. Tegn: vekttap, redusert appetitt, polydipsi, polyuri, cystitt. Diagnose: urinanalyse (protein, blod, sylindre, celler) og biokjemi. Behandling: væskediurese, anabole steroider for å bremse progresjon, sikre konstant tilgang til ferskt vann, unngå stressende situasjoner.

## English

Chronic interstitial nephritis occurs in ageing rodents including hamsters. Signs: weight loss, reduced appetite, polydipsia, polyuria, cystitis. Diagnosis: urinalysis (protein, blood, casts, cells) and biochemistry. Treatment: fluid diuresis, anabolic steroids to slow progression, ensure constant access to fresh water, avoid stressful situations.
