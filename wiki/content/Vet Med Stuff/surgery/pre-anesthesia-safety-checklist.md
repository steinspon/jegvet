# Pre-Anesthesia Safety Checklist

## Before Induction
- Confirm patient identity, procedure, and consent.
- Verify fasting status and pre-op bloodwork review.
- Check ET tube sizes, oxygen supply, and suction readiness.

## Monitoring Setup
1. Pulse oximetry.
2. Blood pressure monitoring.
3. ECG setup.
4. Temperature support plan.

## Team Pause
Perform a verbal safety pause before induction.
