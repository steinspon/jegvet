# Suspected Toxin Intake Triage

## First Contact Questions
- What was ingested, and approximately how much?
- When did exposure happen?
- Current clinical signs (vomiting, tremors, hypersalivation, collapse)?

## Immediate Actions
- Stabilize airway, breathing, and circulation before decontamination decisions.
- Contact poison hotline as needed for product-specific guidance.
- Document product details and batch/photo when possible.

## Red Flags
- Active seizures.
- Progressive neurologic depression.
- Severe cardiovascular instability.
