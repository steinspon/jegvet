Calculation information

Assumptions used in this calculator:

  • Dog Zoletil mix dose: 0.08 ml/kg
  • Cat Zoletil mix dose: 0.10 ml/kg
  • Euthasol / Exagon dose: 0.35 ml/kg
  • Volume (ml) = dose (ml/kg) x body weight (kg)
