Calculation information

  • Medetomidine 1 mg/ml mood groups: 5, 10, 15 ug/kg
  • Dexmedetomidine 0.5 mg/ml mood groups: 2, 5, 7 ug/kg
  • Methadone 10 mg/ml categories: 0.2 and 0.3 mg/kg

  • Formula: ml = (dose mg/kg x body weight kg) / concentration mg/ml
