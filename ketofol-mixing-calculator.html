Calculation information

Assumptions used in this calculator:

  • Mix ratio: 0.9 ml propofol + 0.1 ml ketamine per 1 ml mixture
  • Equivalent for 2 ml syringe: 1.8 ml propofol + 0.2 ml ketamine
  • Equivalent for 5 ml syringe: 4.5 ml propofol + 0.5 ml ketamine
  • Propofol concentration: 10 mg/ml
  • Belatamine (ketamine) concentration: 100 mg/ml
  • Dose target: 0.1 ml/kg slow IV for induction/maintenance
  • Total ketofol dose volume (ml) = body weight (kg) x 0.1 ml/kg
  • Propofol volume within dose = total volume x 0.9
  • Ketamine volume within dose = total volume x 0.1
