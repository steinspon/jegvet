Calculation information

Assumptions used in this calculator:

  • Midazolam dose range: 0.20 to 0.30 mg/kg
  • Midazolam concentration: 5.00 mg/ml
  • Methadone dose range: 0.20 to 0.30 mg/kg
  • Methadone concentration: 10.00 mg/ml
  • Volume range (ml) = ((dose range mg/kg) x body weight kg) / concentration mg/ml
  • Combined IV volume range = midazolam range + methadone range
