Calculation information

Assumptions used in this calculator:

  • Input target concentration is in mg/L
  • Input tablet strength is in mg/tablet
  • Liters water per tablet = tablet strength (mg) / target concentration (mg/L)
  • Displayed result: dL water per tablet = liters per tablet x 10
