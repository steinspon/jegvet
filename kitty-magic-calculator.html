Important information

This sedation is intended for healthy cats where there is no suspicion of heart disease.

All components in the protocol are drawn up in the same syringe and the full volume is given intramuscularly.

Calculation information

Assumptions used in this calculator:

  • Dexmedetomidine concentration: 0.5 mg/ml
  • Methadone concentration: 10 mg/ml
  • Ketamine concentration: 100 mg/ml

Lean cat (BCS 3-4/9)

  • 1.50-5.00 kg: dexmedetomidine 0.02 mg/kg, methadone 0.3 mg/kg, ketamine 3.5 mg/kg
  • 5.01-12.00 kg: dexmedetomidine 0.012 mg/kg, methadone 0.3 mg/kg, ketamine 2.8 mg/kg

Slightly overweight cat (BCS 5-6/9)

  • 1.50-4.00 kg: dexmedetomidine 0.02 mg/kg, methadone 0.3 mg/kg, ketamine 3.5 mg/kg
  • 4.01-5.00 kg: dexmedetomidine 0.015 mg/kg, methadone 0.3 mg/kg, ketamine 3.0 mg/kg
  • 5.01-12.00 kg: dexmedetomidine 0.012 mg/kg, methadone 0.3 mg/kg, ketamine 2.8 mg/kg

Overweight cat (BCS 7-9/9)

  • 1.50-7.10 kg: dexmedetomidine 0.015 mg/kg, methadone 0.3 mg/kg, ketamine 2.5 mg/kg
  • 7.11-12.00 kg: dexmedetomidine 0.010 mg/kg, methadone 0.25 mg/kg, ketamine 2.0 mg/kg
  • Ketamine mood top-up: scared or nervous +0.5 mg/kg; very angry +0.7 mg/kg
  • Final ketamine dose = base ketamine dose by BCS/weight + mood top-up
  • Volume (ml) = (dose mg/kg x body weight kg) / concentration mg/ml
  • Total IM syringe volume = dexmedetomidine volume + methadone volume + ketamine volume
