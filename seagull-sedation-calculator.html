Combined IM Syringe

Enter weight to calculate total IM volume for midazolam + (+) butophanol.

Protocol Notes

Inject IM in chest muscle. Both drugs are combined in the same syringe. Expected sedation onset is within 15 minutes.

Calculation information

Assumptions used in this calculator:

  • Midazolam dose: 1.00 mg/kg
  • Midazolam concentration: 5.00 mg/ml
  • (+) Butophanol dose: 1.00 mg/kg
  • (+) Butophanol concentration: 10.00 mg/ml
  • Volume (ml) = (dose mg/kg x body weight kg) / concentration mg/ml
  • Combined IM volume = midazolam volume + butophanol volume
