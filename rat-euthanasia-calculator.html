Protocol Steps

1. Local anaesthesia
Spray Xylocain 100 mg/ml, 2-3 sprays, on the dorsal neck/shoulder skin area. Optional alternative: Emla cream 25 mg/g + 25 mg/g (increase waiting time to 10-20 minutes).

2. Sedation and anaesthesia
Give subcutaneously in one syringe (blue needle) in the prepared area.

3. Induction
Expected induction time: 2-5 minutes.

4. Euthanasia
When all reflexes are absent, inject final euthanasia drug. Aspirate blood before injection to confirm intravascular position. If localization is uncertain (especially intra-hepatic), volume may be increased slightly.

Footnote
Zoletil-mix is prepared by reconstituting one vial of Zoletil vet powder with 10 ml xylazine + 0.5 ml butorphanol.
