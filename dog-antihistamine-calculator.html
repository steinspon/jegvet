Calculation information

Assumptions used in this calculator:

  • Species: dog
  • Atarax dose: 2.2 mg/kg every 8 hours (TID)
  • Aerius dose: 0.5 mg/kg every 12 hours (BID)
  • Atarax strengths: 10 mg tablet and 25 mg tablet with score line
  • Aerius strengths: 5 mg tablet and 0.5 mg/ml liquid
  • Atarax mg per dose = body weight (kg) x 2.2
  • Aerius mg per dose = body weight (kg) x 0.5
  • Atarax 10 mg is shown as whole tablets only
  • Atarax 25 mg is shown in half-tablet steps
  • Aerius 5 mg is shown as whole tablets only
  • Aerius liquid is only shown as an alternative up to and including 9 kg
