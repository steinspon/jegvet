Calculation information

Assumptions used in this calculator:

  • Induction: fentanyl 0.02 mg/kg (0.05 mg/ml), midazolam 1.0 mg/kg (5 mg/ml), medetomidine 0.05 mg/kg (1 mg/ml)
  • Reversal: naloxone 0.05 mg/kg (0.4 mg/ml), flumazenil 0.1 mg/kg (0.1 mg/ml), atipamezole 0.5 mg/kg (5 mg/ml)
  • Volume (ml) = (dose mg/kg x body weight kg) / concentration mg/ml
  • Combined IM volume = fentanyl + midazolam + medetomidine volumes
